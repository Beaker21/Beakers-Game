Choose your character!

Dark Knight

Dark Knight has higher health and armor, they use death magic to achieve their goals, however their heavy armor causes them to move slowly

Paladin

Paladin have healing magic to keep them alive longer and do massive damage with a Claymore, however they take more damage than a Dark Knight

Ranger

Ranger is quick and agile using two dagger, a bow, and a giant bear to defeat their enemies, they have reduced damage but are quickier then all other opponents

Warlock

Warlock are powerful wizard's that use elemental and dark matter magic to create death and destruction, for those who get in their way. They have less armor then any of the other classes, but who needs armor when you can blow up your enemies from a distance
